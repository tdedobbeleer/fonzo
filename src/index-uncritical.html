Our Philosophy

What do we stand for?

Fonzo, a cosy eatery by us, Piet & Julie, soulmates with a shared passion for food & wine. Fonzo is our personal project so we are constantly innovating, always searching for perfection. Every day we give the best of ourselves to deliver you a lovely experience.

In our restaurant we stay true to:

  • Fresh food 

    Our kitchen is built around fresh vegetables, fresh herbs and tasteful spices. We only make what we love to eat ourselves, straightforward & honest food.

  • Food to share 

    So you can savour a little bit of everything and above all, share the tasting experience in the company of your friends or family.

  • Authentic creations 

    Our dishes are all made in-house with love, always searching for ways to replace industrial produced ingredients and prepare them ourselves, because real food tastes better. We enjoy making original and creative dishes that you cannot find elsewhere.

  • Local ingredients 

    We love local, seasonal and organic ingredients, that is why our menu varies depending on the seasons and arrivals. To support small business like ourselves, we mainly work with local suppliers.
